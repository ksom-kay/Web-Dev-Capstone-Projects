Karen Sakyibea Osei Mensah

Education Supervisor / Lead Facilitator at The MakersPlace

Karen Sakyibea Osei Mensah is a Trade Expert in Robotics Engineering Technology at the Commission for TVET Ghana. Karen Sakyibea also currently works as a SolidWorks Designer and leads the Accra-Ghana SolidWorks User Group. Karen has been Educational Supervisor and Lead Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM) Facilitator at The MakersPlace since February 2018. Karen Sakyibea also serves as the Arduino 4Her Lead there. Previously, Karen was a Micro:bit Ambassador at the Micro:bit Educational Foundation from April 2023 to December 2023. She was the coach for the 2024 FLL National Competition winner (My Reedeener Tech) Karen Sakyibea also worked as a CNC Coordinator at WAF AGRIBUSINESS SOLUTIONS from August 2020 to January 2022, after earning their Bachelor's degree in Agricultural Engineering from the University of Ghana in 2020.
